We cannot publish personal data

Protection of personal data

  • EU's Data Protection Directive, transposed in all Member States with specific rules for the publication of personal data
  • +100 countries around the world have enacted data protection legislation
    • Acts are similar but not the same
  • The OECD publishes a Privacy Framework with guidelines

Personal data

Data to open up

  • Athletes
  • Competitions
  • Venues

Athletes

  • personal info
  • team
  • federation
  • coach
  • results (in competitions)
  • personal bests
  • …

Competitions

  • participants (athletes, federations, teams)
  • schedule
  • (athletes') results
  • records
  • venue
  • …

Venues

  • type of venue (track and field stadium, road/XC courses, etc.
  • location (address, coordinates, …)
  • features (track radius, length, lanes number, etc.)
  • validated disciplines
  • management
  • …

Anonymisation


There are some techniques, but avoiding specific personal fields would be enough.
